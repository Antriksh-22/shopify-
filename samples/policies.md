# Store Knowledge Base

## Returns And Refunds
Customers can request a refund or replacement within 30 days when an item arrives damaged, defective, or materially different from the product description. Ask for an order number and photo evidence before approving the refund.

## Shipping Delays
If a domestic shipment is delayed by more than 5 business days, support should apologize, share the latest tracking status, and offer either store credit or reshipment when tracking has stopped moving.

## VIP Customers
Customers tagged VIP should receive a warm tone, priority support, and a retention offer when the order is above 75 USD.

## Inventory Rules
When projected inventory falls at or below the reorder point, create an inventory task for operations before the next daily purchasing review.

## Fraud Review
High value first orders, chargeback language, or mismatched customer details should be manually reviewed before fulfillment.
